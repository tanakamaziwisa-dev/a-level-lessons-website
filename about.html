About Tanaka

Helping Zimbabwean learners thrive in A-Level exams.

Tutor background

Tanaka is an experienced A-Level tutor with a passion for building confidence, clarity, and exam-ready skill sets. Lessons are informed by real exam trends and personalised to each learner’s strengths.

Teaching approach

  • Structured weekly goals with measurable progress.
  • Active learning with practice questions and feedback.

Values

  • Growth mindset and consistent improvement.
  • Respect for each learner’s pace and goals.
